WHo WE ARE

STEM In Place is a group of high schoolers that provides high-quality, free, interactive, and fun learning opportunities for middle schoolers. We are all volunteers and teach our numerous courses independent of any outside organization or school.

About Us

Let us know by filling out the form to the right. We are looking for high school students who are passionate about STEM topics and enjoy teaching others.

HOW WE TEACH

CLASS-BASED STYLE

Unlike other sites that provide tutoring that are non-interactive, we have a fully interactive curriculum that has been fully tailored to fit a short, but informative summer program. Over the course of our summer program, you will get a head start to the topics that you choose while also being fully engaged.

FULLY ONLINE

Our classes are fully online and utilize the online meeting application, Zoom.

FREE!

All of the courses that we provide are 100% free. We rely on volunteers to provide you courses that are educational, interesting, and fun!
